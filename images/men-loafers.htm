FAQs

Is the loafer a casual shoe?

Among the staples of subtle style, loafers bridge the gap between formal and casual. However, it's important to know that these are more formal shoes. Your business casual looks will be complete with them in that case.

Is it a must to wear socks with loafers?

Unfortunately, no. It appears that the default way to wear loafers is without socks. Chinos and jeans are especially prone to this problem.

Are loafers suitable for wearing with jeans?

In warm seasons, you can wear loafers sockless with shorts and denim. For something a little more classy, wear a shirt and chinos if you want to keep things relaxed

Do loafers look good on a daily basis?

A tuxedo or formal black suit should be worn with these loafers. The shoes have a shiny black upper and a low-cut design. It is perfect for formal events such as formal party, weddings or a ball dance. But they might not look appropriate for everyday use.
